Marisol Moran

Founder & Creative Director of Soul Shine Collections

Marisol Moran is a visionary founder, creative director, and multidimensional entrepreneur redefining the intersection of fashion, wellness, and feminine leadership.

As the founder of Soul Shine Collections, Marisol built a luxury-conscious brand rooted in self-expression, intentional living, and inner radiance. What began as a creative vision evolved into an ecosystem of transformational offerings designed to support women in both their personal growth and professional expansion.

Beyond Soul Shine Collections, Marisol is the creator of:

  • The Goddess Spark Tribe — coaching platform, digital courses, and podcast
  • The Goddess Collection — custom couture gowns and ceremonial designs
  • Design to Delivery — apparel design & sourcing consulting, design strategy, and creative direction

Marisol's work blends business precision with emotional depth. She believes true success is built through both vision and connection — empowering women to rise financially, creatively, and spiritually.

"Ambition with alignment. Luxury with meaning. Leadership with heart."
